PC CONTROL LAYOUT

Move Foward : D
Move Backwards : S
Move Left : A
Move Right : D
Sight Stance : SPACEBAR (hold or toggle)
Aim : Mouse Direction (relative to player)
Fire Primary Weapon : LMB (while in sighted stance)
Use Object : LMB (while moving stance)

Cycle Camera Angle Foward : ]
Cycle Camera Angle Backwards : [
